Rachel Parcell Site Clone

HTML | React | CSS | PostgreSQL | Javascipt | Node

I really like a designer from my area, Rachel Parcell, and I looked at her website one day and I saw the different ways I could make it better. I made it mobile responsive, because a lot of shoppers are on phones. She has a very prominent Instagram presence, and really needs a mobile friendly site, so her followers can go from her cute Instagram page to her cute mobile site.

I gave it a simpler design as well. I saw that it wasn't quite as simple as it could have been, and I decided to leave a couple of the things she has on her live site out of my clone, because I felt that those things were not necessary.

I love her designs, and I think the color scheme and the basic layout is really cute. I kept the cute pink color, and I kept the peony idea, and I used all of her designs and her descriptions and prices to keep the essence of her and the great designer that she is.
